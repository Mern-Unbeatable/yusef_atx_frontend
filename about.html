Founder story

A JOURNEY TO RECLAIM
MY IDENTITY.

In 2018, ten days before my 30th birthday, a high-speed collision changed everything. On paper, I looked fine. Internally, I was a stranger to myself. A traumatic brain injury sidelined my life, turning once-simple tasks into exhausting hurdles and forcing me away from the sports and rituals that defined me.

I was told to "be patient," but as an engineer, I couldn't just wait for a system to fix itself. I knew my body was malfunctioning, even if the pain was invisible to the outside world. I spent years navigating my own recovery, applying the same methodical, systems-thinking approach I used in my engineering career to my own healing.

I stopped looking at my injury as a "waiting game" and started looking at it as a challenge to be solved. My recovery wasn't just about healing an injury; it was a journey to reclaim my identity.

Our mission

Dedicated to your comeback.

That experience changed my perspective on health forever. I realized that movement isn't just a hobby, it's how we stay aligned with who we are.

Athletic Alignment Labs was born out of that struggle. My mission is to ensure that no one in Austin has to settle for a "new normal" that keeps them on the sidelines. By combining an engineer's precision with high-level manual therapy, I've built a process that bridges the gap between "just fine" and peak performance. We are here to help you stop managing your symptoms and start living your life again.
